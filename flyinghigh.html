PARAGLIDING

Experience the feeling of freedom in the company of an experienced and accredited pilot. See from above the beautiful landscapes, feel the wind and smell the clouds with the birds for company. Share the space with other paragliders flowing with them in the blue of the sky, on a huge palette of colors. The flight will be made in a two-way paraglider, always in the company of a pilot accredited by FPVL, with all the necessary equipment to do the flight baptisms. It is recommended to all participants, take warm and comfortable clothes, mountain boots or similar.

PARATRIKE

During a bi-rent flight in Paratrike you can take your feet off the ground and live this unique experience! Paratrike is a paragliding mode made with a rigid and motor structure, which allows greater comfort and control throughout the flight. After a short briefing on the flight and equipment, the adrenaline starts with the gentle start up the runway until you feel the ground escape and start flying.

SKYDIVE

Skydiving is very easy. You'll wear a costume, followed by a theoretical presentation of the activity (briefing) for 5 to 10 minutes so you can finally be ready to get on the plane. When you reach your chosen altitude, you'll jump with the very experienced instructor, who will be using equipment designed to take two people. The free fall will allow you to have the feeling of flying in the skies of alentejo, reaching a speed of about 200km/h. When you open the parachute, you will feel a lightness in both the body and soul!

SOFT-COASTEERING

Soft Coasteering or in other words «Flying Into Water», is a multiactivity, since it encompasses, Trekking, Swimming, Caving, Small Climbing (no need for ropes) and Jumps to the Sea... In fact, many jumps to the crystal clear waters of the coast of Serra da Arrábida.The experienced instructors will ensure the best safety conditions for an unforgettable experience!
